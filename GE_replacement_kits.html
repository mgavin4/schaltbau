GE DS303B C193 Contactor Replacement Kit

Drop-in replacement kit for most of the GE DS303B series of DC contactors that is used in many industrial applications such as steel mills, cranes, material handling and railway equipment. Our replacement kit includes a contactor, mounting plate and associated hardware. The Schaltbau C193 contactor included in this kit is rated at 1000V, can handle currents up to 50A and is available with coil voltages of 24, 36, 48, 72, 80, or 110V DC.

GE DS303B C195 Contactor Replacement Kit

Drop-in replacement kit for most of the heavy duty GE DS303B series of DC contactors that is used in demanding industrial applications such as steel mills, cranes, material handling and railway equipment. Kit includes a contactor, mounting plate and associated hardware. The Schaltbau C195 contactor in this kit is rated at 1000V, can handle currents up to 250A and is available with coil voltages of 24, 36, 48, 60, 72, 80, 96, 110, or 220V DC.

GE IC2800 C195 Contactor Replacement Kit

Drop-in replacement kit for selected GE IC2800 series of general duty and mill-duty DC contactors. Typical general duty applications include DC industrial control of machine tools and textile machinery. Mill-duty applications include steel mills, cranes, material handling and railway equipment. Our replacement kit includes a contactor, mounting plate and associated hardware. The Schaltbau C195 contactor included in this kit is rated at 1000V, can handle currents up to 250A and is available with coil voltages of 24, 36, 48, 60, 72, 80, 96, 110, or 220V DC.

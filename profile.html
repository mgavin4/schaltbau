Company Profile

Schaltbau North America has created this website to highlight our DC contactor product line. Our product offering ranges from battery voltages up to 3000V with current handling capacities from 10A to 1100A. Schaltbau's devices are designed to withstand harsh environments and have been in use for decades in many demanding applications including heavy industry and transit-related systems.

Schaltbau was established over 80 years ago in Munich, Germany. Our product lines include contactors, snap-action switches, connectors and electrical systems for railway applications. We continue to maintain manufacturing and assembly facilities for all our products at two locations in Germany and are certified to the highest quality standards including ISO and IRIS.

Based in Hauppauge, NY, our North American location can provide value-added capabilities such as technical support, engineering services, light assembly, kitting and distribution.

Technical Sales Manager

Location:

» Irvine, California

Job Summary:

» Reviews requirements of Industrial customers and helps develop customized solutions using Schaltbau products.

» Uses specialized knowledge of New Generation Energy applications such as Solar, Wind, Energy Storage / Battery Systems to generate sales of Schaltbau products.

» Provides technical support on Schaltbau’s products.

Duties and Responsibilities:

» Reports to the Vice President of Engineering and Technical Sales.

» Reviews technical drawings and specifications of Industrial applications to ensure that Schaltbau solutions meet or exceed customer requirements.

» Meets with customers to review technical requirements and optimizes proposals from Schaltbau to address their needs.

» Coordinates with Sales Management and the Product Groups in the corporate office to target suitable customers for Schaltbau products.

» Develops documentation and application notes highlighting the use of Schaltbau’s products in Industrial applications.

» Provides after-sales support.

Education:

» University degree (or equivalent work experience) in Electrical, Mechanical or Industrial Engineering.

Experience:

» 2 to 5 years of experience as an Application Engineer for products related to the New Generation Energy market.

Experience:

» Excellent organizational, interpersonal, and communication skills.

» Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.

» Good knowledge and understanding of electrical schematics.

» Proficient in the use of Microsoft Office software.

» Knowledge of Autodesk CAD software an asset.

» Willingness to travel domestically and internationally about 30 – 40% of the time.
